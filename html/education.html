Education

Due to the transferrable nature of my father's job, i had the opportunity to visit and stay in different parts of the country. My education too played a really essential role in who i am today.

APS Port Blair

I spent my preschool in APS Port Blair. I still cherish the white sand and blue oceans of Andaman and Nicobar.

Etasi Timpany School

I did my 1st to 4th here.One of the best Schools in Vizag and had huge sprawling playgrounds

Holy Innocents HS

I did my 5th and 6th here and Ooty has been one of my favourite places till now.The cold weather and clear skies coupled with low population literally translates to heaven.

DPS Vizag

I did my 7th here and it helped me develop a lot of leadership skills.Also we had a view of the sea by our class.

CCA School

I did My 8th and 9th.Being new to northern culture , initially it was a little awkward but soon good friends helped me adapt and we had a wild time.Also this ignited my love for quizzing.

APS Kirkee

I did my first year of boards here ie 10th grade.Had a lot of fun and made some life-long friends

FIITJEE Pune

Just like any other student after 10th grade , i too pursued PCM. Met some really great friends and teachers along the way.

IIITH

Almost finished my first year of college with no interaction with coursemates.Fell in love with computer sciences here.
